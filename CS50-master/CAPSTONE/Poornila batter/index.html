About Us

Serving Since 1995

Our Story

Bringing the authentic taste of home to your kitchen with healthy, traditional flours and batters.

Born from a love for traditional Indian cooking and the taste of home, our brand started with a simple goal – to bring authentic, healthy, and high-quality flours and batters to every kitchen. We believe in preserving traditional recipes while making them convenient for modern families. Every product we make carries the essence of home, trust, and goodness, so you can cook with confidence and enjoy the flavors you grew up with.
